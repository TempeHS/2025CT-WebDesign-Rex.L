Remote Controlled Technology

Remote controlled technology has many uses in the world. Such include remote controlled drones, garage door openers, lights, etc

Remote controlled technology is also used in military UAVs and has helped save a lot of lives in terms of not having a pilot in the vehicle and in possible danger. While most of the communication to the drone from the main control centre is through satelites, a portion of the controlling is through longer range remote control technology





How does remote control work?

So first, what is infrared technology and how does it work? Broadly speaking, there are two types of infrared technology: those that detect infrared radiation and those that send information via infrared radiation (called wireless infrared technology).Infrared technology that detects infrared often involves sensors that turn detected infrared radiation into usable information.

How do remote radio control systems work?

Radio remote control (RF remote control) is used to control distant objects using a variety of radio signals transmitted by the remote control device. As a complementary method to infrared remote controls, the radio remote control is used with electric garage door or gate openers, automatic barrier systems, burglar alarms and industrial automation systems.
